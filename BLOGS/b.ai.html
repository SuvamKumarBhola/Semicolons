Artificial Intelligence

Artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) are two terms that dominate tech headlines. But what exactly do they mean, and how are they impacting our lives? In this blog post, we'll break down these concepts, explore their applications, and discuss the exciting future they hold.

The Early Beginnings

The concept of AI dates back to ancient times, with myths and legends featuring automatons imbued with human-like intelligence. Yet, it wasn't until the 20th century that AI emerged as a formal field of study. In 1956, the Dartmouth Conference marked the birth of AI as researchers gathered to explore the possibility of creating machines capable of intelligent behavior.
